About Gambino

Childish Gambino is the alter ego of multi-hyphenate AMerican entertainer, Donald Glover, who is also known for acting roles on television shows like Community and the Emmy- and Golden Globe- winning Atlanta, as well as the film SOlo: A star wars story. As Childish Gambino, Glover refined his off-kilter brand of hip-hop in his 2013 breakthrough album “Because the internet”, before making a sharp turn toward R&B-inspired funk, which won him a Grammy for best traditional r&b performance in 2017 for the single “Redbone”.
